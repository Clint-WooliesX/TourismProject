Getting here



Hover to enlarge

Wisemans Ferry is located approximately 60km from the Sydney CBD. Driving is the best option.


Trains run from the city to Richmond. From there it is recommended to hire a car for the day to explore the area. The Ferries at the various river crossings are free of charge


Access to Dural and Wisemans Ferry is best via Hills M2 Motorway to the Castle Hill exit. Follow the Metroad 2 signs to Castle Hill Road, then follow Tourist Drive 15 along Old Northern Road through Dural all the way to Wisemans Ferry (approx 90 minutes from the city).


The area has a number of scenic drives and is a favoured location for car and motorcycle enthusiasts. Winding roads and beautiful vistas will keep you coming back again and again.

Ferry hours

The Webbs Creek Ferry at Wisemans Ferry crosses the Hawkesbury River just south of the junction of the Macdonald River.


Operates 24/hrs 7days a week.


only closed for maintenance on the 1st Tuesday of each month from 9.30am till 12.00pm.
