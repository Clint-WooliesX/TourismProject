Getting here

Wisemans Ferry is approximately 60km from the Sydney CBD. Driving is the best option.


Access to Dural and Wisemans Ferry is best via Hills M2 Motorway to the Castle Hill exit. Follow the Metroad 2 signs to Castle Hill Road then follow Tourist Drive 15 along Old Northern Road through Dural all the way to Wisemans Ferry (approx 90 minutes from the city).


The area has a number of senic drives and is a favoured location for car and motorcycle enthusiasts. winding road and beautiful vistas will keep you coming back again and agian.
